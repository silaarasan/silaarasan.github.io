Chekku Oil / Marachekku Oil / Chekku Ennai / Marachekku Ennai

Chekku Oil / Chekku Ennai is also known as Cold Pressed Oil in english.

Cold pressing (Chekku Oil / Marachekku Ennai) is a process which involved pressing and extraction of oils from the seeds or nuts or vegetables without adding any chemicals or preservatives at no heat at all.

The shelf life of chekku oil is just 3-4 months which has fresh aroma and adds additional taste to your food.
